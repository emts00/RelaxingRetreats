Meet the team

Who are we?

We are a small team of people who all share a passion for travel. We have visited many spectacular places around the world and created this website in 2019 to allow you to do the same. Travelling gives us the opportunity to explore new cultures, try new foods and see things we have never seen before however, we understand that this can be very costly these days and this is why we work hard to find you the best deals at the lowest prices. We aim to make your trip away as exciting and easy as possible.

Where have you travelled?

We have been lucky enough to travel to over 80 different destinations, including Thailand, Japan, New York, Paris, Australia, Italy, Greece and many more. We share our experiences with you to provide some top tips and tricks to make planning your trip as easy as possible. We also include all of the "must see" attractions in each location.
