Japan

Go on an adventure of a lifetime

Tokyo

A vibrant tapestry of ancient traditions and cutting-edge innovation, this city will whisk you away on an electrifying adventure! Marvel at the awe-inspiring skyline as you cross the iconic Shibuya Crossing, where a symphony of colorful chaos unfolds. Immerse yourself in the serenity of the Meiji Shrine, where time seems to stand still amidst the bustling city. Savor the freshest sushi at Tsukiji Fish Market, a paradise for food enthusiasts. Then, ascend Tokyo Tower and witness the city transform into a dazzling sea of neon lights. Tokyo promises an exhilarating journey through its captivating blend of old-world charm and futuristic marvels!

Osaka

Welcome to Osaka, the vibrant and exhilarating city that will ignite your senses and steal your heart! Dive into the neon-lit streets of Dotonbori, where sizzling street food beckons with its irresistible aromas. Indulge in the mouthwatering delights of okonomiyaki, takoyaki, and melt-in-your-mouth sushi at bustling markets. Immerse yourself in the rich history of Osaka Castle and explore the hidden alleyways of Shinsekai. Unleash your inner adventurer at Universal Studios Japan, where thrilling rides and beloved characters await. Whether you're a foodie, thrill-seeker, or culture enthusiast, Osaka promises an electrifying journey that will leave you craving for more!

Aoshima

Prepare to be whisked away to the enchanting Aoshima Island, where feline wonders reign supreme! Known as the famous Cat Island, this purrfect paradise offers a unique and unforgettable experience for cat lovers. As you step foot onto the island, you'll be greeted by a flurry of friendly felines, meowing their warm welcomes. Explore the idyllic landscapes, with cats lounging in the sun-drenched streets and chasing butterflies in the lush gardens. Bask in the joy of cuddling and playing with these adorable creatures, creating cherished memories that will make your heart melt. Aoshima Island is a true haven for cat aficionados, a dream come true in a whisker!
